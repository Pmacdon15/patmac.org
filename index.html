A little bit about my self

Hello and good day, My name is Patrick MacDonald. I'm making this site to aid in acquiring a job in the tech industry. I will start by explaining about my first career and how I got interested in Software Development. I started my driving career when I was 22 years old. I had a string of hard jobs for little pay and I was looking for a change. At the time I was young and very outgoing and believed I could conquer the world. So with that attitude I started my career as a truck driver because I thought it would be fun to drive large trucks(it was).

While being proud of the career I built I was also very tired of it. Although I had a fairly good wage, I was unable to get a head. I was always living pay check to pay check not able to save very much each month. I would also get stuck at work when something like a tire would blow up or a truck would break down. I would have to wait for a mechanic to come then having to ask a family friend to pick up my step daughter from school. Also being outside in the elements was not fun. I know some people have it worse and being outside for an hour or so is not that bad but I thought there had to be another way.

That's when I started an introspection and asked myself what is it I want to do with my life.Then I remembered how much I loved programming in high school and how it made me think and feel. I decided that I would get my GED and apply for software developmentAt Bow Valley College. It took me about a month to get my GED and after that I applied for the program and was accepted but placed on a wait list. In the mean time I took a few classes on udemy just to make sure that software development was a proper fit for me and to my surprise I loved it. January rolled around and I was able to start my classes at Bow Valley. This brings us to now where I am now entering my 3rd term and I am looking for an internship or some sort of part time job in the software development.
